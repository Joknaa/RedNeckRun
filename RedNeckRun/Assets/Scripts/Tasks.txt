Game Flow/ theme :- 
1. Level starts, you can move your character from left to right. Character automatically walks forward on
a straight path.
2. The goal is to collect different type of pickable objects and convert your form to better suit the selected identity.
3. In the beginning of level, there will be 2 gate one for redneck and one for the hipster, and player can
select according to his choice.
4. There will be few collectable objects on the way, few for redneck and few for hipster, if
player selects the right pickable, it will increase he character bar and once full it will change the character,
if you pick the wrong pickable, the bar can decrease and the character can downgrade as well.
5. Apart from collectable objects, there will be few gates on the way with different available options like
(flower or Rifle, sports car or pickup truck, Apple Watch or antique wrist watch etc.), if player selects the
correct gate according to the selected theme he will get few points or if player selects the wrong gate he
will loses some points.
6. Also, there will be a bar on top of the character that will reflect points, Once the bar is completely filled
the character will upgrade according to the selected theme (Redneck or hipster) or the character will
downgrade once the bar is completely empty.
